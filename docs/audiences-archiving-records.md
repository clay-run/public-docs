# Archiving records in Audiences

Soft-delete records from a segment without permanently removing them.

Archiving lets you remove records from active workflows and segments in Audiences without deleting them outright. Archived records are excluded from every segment and workflow they previously belonged to but remain accessible in the `Archived` section of the sidebar so you can review or restore them later.

## Archive records

You can archive all records in a segment from within that segment.

1.  In your Audiences workspace, open the segment whose records you want to archive.
2.  Click the segment header menu (the `⋯` overflow button) and select `Archive records`.
    -   You can also access this option from the overflow menu next to the segment name in the sidebar.
3.  A confirmation modal appears showing the number of records that will be archived. Check the box to confirm you understand the action, then click `Archive`.

**Note:** Archiving records is a destructive action. Once archived, records are immediately removed from all segments and Audience workflows they were part of. The segment (list) itself is not deleted — only the records inside it are archived. If you also want to remove the segment, delete it separately.

## View archived records

Archived records appear in the `Archived` section at the bottom of the Audiences sidebar. Click `Archived` to browse all archived records across your workspace. You can filter within this view to narrow down to a specific set of records.

## Restore archived records

Restoring an archived record brings it back into Audiences so it can participate in segments and workflows again.

1.  In the Audiences sidebar, click `Archived`.
2.  Find the records you want to restore — use the available filters to narrow the list if needed.
3.  Select the records and click `Restore`.

Restored records re-enter Audiences as active records. They are not automatically re-added to the segments they were removed from; segment membership is determined by your segment filters at the time of each sync.

## FAQs

### Does archiving delete the segment?

No. Archiving removes the records inside a segment from active workflows and segments, but the segment itself remains intact. To delete the segment, use the `Delete` option from the segment's overflow menu separately.

### Are archived records billed?

Archived records do not count toward your active record quota.

### Can I archive records from the sidebar without opening the segment first?

Yes — the sidebar overflow menu next to each segment name includes an `Archive records` option. Selecting it navigates you to that segment and opens the confirmation modal so you can review the records before confirming.
