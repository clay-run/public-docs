# Find leads

Clay's unified starting point for finding companies and people.

**Find leads** is a dedicated page in the Clay app that brings all of Clay's lead-discovery tools together in one place. Instead of jumping directly into individual source configurations, Find leads lets you choose the right data source for your use case, describe what you're looking for using chat, and pick up where you left off with past searches.

## Accessing Find leads

Click **Find leads** in the left sidebar of the Clay app.

## Companies and People tabs

Find leads opens on the **Companies** tab by default. Use the tabs at the top of the page to switch between:

-   **Companies** — Discover companies using Clay's database or third-party sources.
-   **People** — Find contacts using Clay's database or third-party sources.

## Source cards

Each tab shows a set of source cards representing the data sources available for that search type. Click a card to open the search configuration for that source. Available sources include:

-   **Clay** — Clay's proprietary company and people database (CPJ data). The recommended default for broad searches. See [Find Companies in Clay](https://university.clay.com/docs/find-companies) and [Find People in Clay](https://university.clay.com/docs/find-people-overview) for full filter documentation.
-   **OpenMart** — Specialized for finding SMB leads. See [OpenMart integration](https://university.clay.com/docs/openmart-integration).
-   **HG Insights** — Enterprise-grade technographic data for building lists by technology usage. Requires an HG Insights account. See [HG Insights integration](https://university.clay.com/docs/hg-insights-integration-overview).

Additional sources may be available depending on your plan and workspace configuration.

## Chat

The chat box on Find leads lets you describe what you're looking for in plain language. Clay uses your workspace's business context to offer pre-populated suggestions — for example, proposing searches that match your typical buyer profile. Submit a description and Clay will configure a search you can review before running it as a source.

## Past searches

Below the source cards, the **Past searches** section shows your recent and saved search criteria. Use the segmented control to switch between:

-   **Recents** — Automatically saved whenever you create or edit a search source.
-   **Saved by you** — Searches you have explicitly saved with a name and description.
-   **Workspace** — Searches saved by anyone in your workspace.

Click any past search to reload its filters and run it as a new source.

For more on saving and reusing searches, see [Saved searches](https://university.clay.com/docs/saved-searches).
